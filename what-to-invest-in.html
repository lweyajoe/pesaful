What To Invest In

3rd February, 20225 min read4 comments

So what do we invest in Kenya?

A few examples of investment opportunities and ideas that young working Kenyans can start tapping into:

  1. MMF’s Money Market funds, like Britam and Cytonn, these ones even have apps and you can deposit monthly via MPESA, some offer around 9% to 11% Check our money market fund here.
  2. Savings Accountse KCB Goal Savings account that offers 10% interest and M-AKiba.
  3. REITS - Like the Stanlib I-REIT, gives you exposure to income from Real estate … you buy it as shares on the NSE. You can create an account to trade at the NSE here.
  4. Buy Dividend Yielding Shares/Stocks on the NSE. You can read about that here.
  5. Buy into Government treasuries/Bonds like the day, 182-day e.t.c.
  6. Take up a savings/investment plan with an insurer like Britam/Jubilee/ICEA.
  7. Consider an Individual Pension Plan offered by Insurers
  8. You can invest in Global Markets/Indices/Funds through Standard Chartered i.e SC Mobile app offers this. You need a bank account and invest a minimum of USD 100.
  9. Buy into Corporate Bonds/Rights issues.
  10. Contribute on the voluntary scheme of NSSF at least Ksh 1,000 if u can. The Voluntary Scheme has higher interest earned on contributions.
  11. If your employer offers pension, contribute on the highest percentage of the basic salary scale because the employer matches or contributes double your pension contribution (Varies with employer).
